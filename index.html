Questions answered

Things people ask us

Can't find your answer? Email us at [email protected]

What types of barriers can Wildvue remove?

Fences, cage bars, wire mesh, aquarium glass, safari vehicle windows, you name it. If something is between you and the animal, Wildvue is built to handle it. We're always improving.

Will the animal still look natural?

Yes. Wildvue is built specifically for wildlife photos, so it understands things like fur texture, feathers, and motion blur. In most cases you genuinely can't tell anything was ever in the way.

Is Wildvue free?

Yes! There will be a free option with a set of credits each month, perfect for casual use. If you're shooting a lot, the Pro plan gives you unlimited enhancements.

Is my original photo kept private?

Completely. Your photos are processed and then deleted from our servers within 24 hours. We never use your photos for anything without you explicitly opting in.

Does it work for aquarium photos too?

Absolutely. Aquarium glass, murky water reflections, debris floating in the tank. Wildvue handles all of it. Some of our favorite before and afters are underwater shots.

When is the app launching?

We're targeting iOS and Android in the coming months. Waitlist members get access before everyone else, with invites going out in batches. Sign up and you'll be near the front of the line.
